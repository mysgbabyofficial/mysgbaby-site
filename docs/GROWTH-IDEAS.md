# What else would benefit new parents — researched ideas

Researched July 2026 (sources cited below). **The single biggest opportunity: the site currently ends at delivery — but a new parent's needs, searches, and spending *explode* after birth.** Extending from *pregnancy* into the *"fourth trimester" and first year* is where the most value (and traffic, and affiliate potential) lives. Everything below fits the zero-budget, "signpost + cite, not medical advice" posture.

## The gap in one picture
Singapore's own **LifeSG "Welcoming your newborn"** hub lists what parents scramble for *after* birth: birth registration, bringing baby home, newborn sleep/feeding, postnatal recovery, childhood vaccinations, confinement nanny, childminding, childcare & subsidies. MySGBaby covers almost none of this yet. *(Source: life.gov.sg/family-parenting/when-your-child-is-born.)*

---

## Tier 1 — highest value, on-brand, low-liability (do first)

### 1. "After baby arrives" admin checklist & timeline
A dated checklist for the first weeks: **birth registration** (within 14 days), **citizenship/passport** (newborn passport-fee waiver), **Baby Bonus + CDA enrolment**, **MediSave Grant for Newborns** ($5,000, automatic), **MediShield Life** from birth. Pure signposting to LifeSG/ICA/MSF — high utility, near-zero liability. *(Source: LifeSG.)*

### 2. Confinement cost planner & guide ⭐
The biggest cost parents under-budget, and a perfect fit for your cost-planning strength. **2026 figures (cited):** confinement nanny **S$3,500–S$5,500** (CNY $6,000+), confinement centre **S$8,000–S$20,000+**, meals **S$1,500–S$3,000**, postnatal massage **S$300–S$800**, lactation consult **S$150–S$400** — total **S$4,000–S$20,000+**, and **MediSave does not cover it.** Add a simple budget calculator (nanny vs centre vs DIY). *(Sources: bankingcredit.com.sg, newbubs.sg, 2026.)*

### 3. Childhood vaccination schedule (+ simple tracker)
Singapore's **NCIS** runs birth→11y; **Diphtheria & Measles are legally mandatory** (fine up to $10,000); ~10+ doses; polyclinic cost **$10–$30** after subsidy for citizens. A schedule page + a localStorage tracker ("next jab due") is genuinely useful and low-liability (signpost HealthHub/HPB). *(Source: halfhalfparenting, 2026.)*

### 4. Postnatal recovery & support directory
Extend the compassionate tone of your Loss Support page. Signpost: **KKH/NUH home-nurse visits**, **lactation consultants** (IBCLC ~$150–$300/visit), **KidSTART** (lower-income), **Family Service Centres**, and mental-health/postnatal-depression lines — **HPB Breastfeeding Helpline**, **AWARE 1800-777-5555**, **IMH 6389 2222**, **SOS 1767**. Pure directory, high care value. *(Source: heybaby.sg.)*

## Tier 2 — strong follow-ups

### 5. Extend the Benefits Centre to the first 7 years
You already have the benefits calculator — extend it beyond birth to **infant/childcare subsidies** (up to ~$1,710/month), **WMCR / QCR / Parenthood Tax Rebate / Grandparent Caregiver Relief**, **Child LifeSG Credits**, and the **Large Families Scheme**. *(Sources: LifeSG, heybaby.sg.)*

### 6. Childcare / infant-care guide + "when to apply"
Waitlists are long; parents must plan early. Explain subsidy tiers, enrolment timing, and the government **childminding pilot**. Signpost ECDA/LifeSG.

### 7. Newborn care basics (the "fourth trimester")
Feeding, sleep, jaundice, and "when to call the doctor" — summarised and linked to HealthHub/KKH, behind the medical disclaimer. Add this as a **new stage in your interactive explorer** ("Fourth Trimester / First weeks").

### 8. Simple parent tools (client-side, zero-cost, high retention)
Due-date calculator, **kick counter**, **contraction timer**, and a **feeding / sleep / diaper tracker** (all localStorage). These are the features that make pregnancy apps sticky and bring people back daily.

---

## Tier 3 — bigger / defer

### 9. Community / peer support
Research says peer support is the **#1 factor in new-parent wellbeing**, and your closest local competitor (**Pregnant and Popped**) is essentially a community. A full forum is heavy — start zero-budget by **signposting trusted SG communities** + a curated "questions parents are asking this week" FAQ. *(Sources: heybaby.sg, pregnantandpopped.com.)*

### 10. Product guides & registry (monetisation)
Curated newborn-essentials guides with price ranges + **affiliate links** (Shopee/Lazada/Amazon SG) — the natural revenue layer once traffic exists.
